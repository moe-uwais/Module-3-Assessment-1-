Grilled Salmon

INGREDIENTS:


    Salmon
  • 4 skin-on salmon fillets


    • Marinade
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp fresh cut rosemary
  • 1/2 tsp sweet paprika
  • salt and pepper




INSTRUCTIONS:



Marinate
  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the olive oil and lemon juice
  2. Stir the minced garlic, dried oregano, rosemary, and paprika into the marinade.
  3. Place the salmon in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over it.
  4. Let it marinate for at least 15 minutes in the fridge for at least 15 minutes.


Grilling
  1. PREHEAT the grill or griddle pan to medium-high.
  2. REMOVE the salmon from the marinade and pat it dry.
  3. Place the salmon on the grill or griddle pan and cook for 4-6 minutes per side, or until it flakes easily with a fork.
