Chicken Skewers Recipe

INGREDIENTS:


    Chicken
  • 1 KG of chicken breast.


    • Marinade
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp fresh cut rosemary
  • 1/2 tsp sweet paprika
  • salt and pepper


    • Vegetables (optional)
  • Zucchini slices
  • Red onion chunks
  • Bell peppers(optional)

INSTRUCTIONS:



Marinate
  1. Combine marinade ingredients and toss with chicken.
  2. Let it sit for at least 20 minutes (up to 24 hours) in the fridge.


Prep Skewers
  1. If using a wooden skewers, soak them in water for 30 minutes to prevent it from burning.


Assemble
  1. Thread chicken and vegetables onto skewers, alternating as desired leave small gaps for heat circulation.


Grill
  1. Heat the grill or griddle pan to medium-high.
  2. Cook for 8-10 minutes.
  3. Turn it every 2-3 minutes until the internal temperature reaches 75°C (167°F).


Rest
  1. Allow chicken to rest for 5 minutes before serving to keep suculent.
