Baked Chicken Wings

INGREDIENTS:


    Chicken
  • 1 KG of chicken wings.


    • Marinade
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp sweet paprika
  • salt and pepper


INSTRUCTIONS:



Marinate
  1. Combine marinade ingredients and toss with chicken.
  2. Let it sit for at least 20 minutes (up to 24 hours) in the fridge.


Prepare
  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  3. Pat the marinated chicken wings dry with paper towels.


Bake
  1. Place the chicken wings on the prepared baking tray in a single layer.
  2. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
  3. If desired, add vegetables to the tray halfway through baking.


Rest & Serve
  1. Allow chicken wings to rest for 5 minutes before serving to keep them juicy.
  2. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
  3. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce or as a main dish.
