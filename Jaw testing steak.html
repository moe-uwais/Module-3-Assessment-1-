Jaw Testing Steak

INGREDIENTS:


    Steak
  • 1 KG of beef steak.


    • Marinade
  • Sweet and sour sauce (1/4 cup)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • rosemary
  • salt and pepper


INSTRUCTIONS:



Marinate
  1. Combine marinade ingredients and toss with steak.
  2. Let it sit for at least 20 minutes (up to 24 hours) in the fridge.


Prepare
  1. Pat the marinated steak dry with paper towels.
  2. Line a baking tray with parchment paper..


Bake
  1. Place the steak on the prepared baking tray in a single layer.
  2. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
  3. Check the internal temperature with a meat thermometer; it should be 60-65°C (140-150°F) for medium-rare.


Rest & Serve
  1. Allow the steak to rest for 5-10 minutes before serving to keep it juicy.
  2. Let the steak reach room temperature before serving for even better texture.
